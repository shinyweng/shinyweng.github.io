Hi there! 👋 I am a Master's student in Computer Science (MSCS) at Stanford 🌲, specializing in Artificial Intelligence. Previously, I graduated from UC Berkeley 🐻 with Honors, where I double-majored in Computer Science and Statistics 💻📈.
